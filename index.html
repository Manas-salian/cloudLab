Department Overview

Established in 2007 with an intake of 60 students, the Department of Computer Science & Engineering has scaled rapidly to meet global software industry requirements. Today, our intake stands at 240 students. The core B.E. Program is proudly accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) through 2026.
